Dear Sir/Madam,

We are happy to invite your institute to nominate Research scholars for the
TCS Research Scholar Program *Batch 20* effective from *1st July 2026 –
30th June 2030.*

*About TCS Research Scholar Program*:
Tata Consultancy Services Limited (TCS) through its TCS Research Scholar
Program (RSP) provides scholarship to full-time registered PhD scholars to
encourage quality multidisciplinary research activities in areas ranging
from computer engineering and science, information systems and technology,
software engineering and other disciplines which utilize computing.

The TCS Research Scholar Program was started with the aim of nurturing the
computer science research related ecosystem in India. We are very proud to
have facilitated significant increase in the Intellectual Capital of the
County over the last 15 years plus through our TCS RSP program by
supporting over 422 Scholars from 50 premier institutes across the country.
Scholars have benefited from mentorships and presentations to TCS
Researchers.

Noteworthy are :

   - Contribution to the Academic Institutions: Significant number of
   professionals (100 plus) hold academic positions, reflecting a strong
   emphasis on teaching and research in STEM fields.  They are now working in
   various senior Academic roles in the leading Universities of India, Asia,
   the US, and Europe.
   - Many individuals (nearly 200) transition between academia and
   industry, indicating a fluid career path that values both practical and
   theoretical expertise.
   - The presence of roles in both sectors showcase a diverse skill set
   focused on advanced technologies, AI, data science, and engineering.​Our
   successful scholars in Industry are part of the world's leading Technology
   Companies , Financial Institutions, Startups and Leading R&D Labs from
   across the geographies.


*Features:*

TCS will sponsor selected scholars for *four years* (or until submission of
thesis for PhD, whichever is earlier).

*Scholarship Details:*

Period: A scholar who is selected for the scholarship will be given a
stipend for a maximum of four years, or submission of thesis for PhD,
whichever is earlier.

Stipend: A stipend of *Rs. 91,000 per month for the first and second year*
and *Rs. 97,500 per month for the third year* and *Rs. 1,26,750 per month
for the fourth year*. In the fourth year of the TCS Research Scholar
Program, the Scholars are eligible for doing 1 semester i.e., 6 months in a
top university abroad with the help of Institute’s foreign relationships to
which the Scholars will be paid *Rupees Ninety Thousand (Rs. 90,000/-)* in
addition to their 6 months stipend.

*Other Incentives:*

   1. Assignment of TCS Mentor from TCS Research and innovation team (R&I)
   soon after selection to TCS Research Scholar Program.
   2. Benefits worth *INR 9,02,000 over 4 years* for supporting actual
   expenses for (a) Laptops and Books up to *INR 2 Lakhs* (b) National
   Conference for Scholar up to *INR 0.5 lakhs* (c) International
   Conference for Scholar up to *INR 2.32 Lakhs* (d) 1-time International
   Conference for Guide up to *INR 1.5 lakhs* (e)  Travel and settling cost
   for sabbatical to foreign university to eligible scholar up to *INR 2.2
   Lakhs* (f) Visit to TCS Innovation Labs up to *INR 0.5 Lakhs*. The
   support is limited to *Rupees Two Lakhs (Rs. 2,00,000/-) per year for
   the first and second year* and *Rupees Two Lakhs Fifty-One Thousand (Rs.
   2,51,000/-) for the remaining two years* and *is non-transferable to
   following year if unused*. The above benefits can be used only once
   up-to maximum allowed cap only over the period of 4 years. Scholars are not
   allowed to be used same grant multiple times.


TCS and the Institute will enter MoU (Rs. 600 stamp paper to be purchased
by the institute) after TCS announces list of selected scholar/s of the
Institute .

Final Draft of MoU to be printed will be shared with the institute after
the announcement of results (Announcements expected to be done by or before
July 2026)



*Eligibility of Applicants: *

This scheme is open to candidates who have been selected by your Institute.

   1. Registered for a full-time PhD program.
   2. Within 28 years of age at the time of application
   3. Completed 2 semesters and should be in 2nd year of Ph.D having a
   brilliant academic background or 3rd year in case of dual degree program.
   4. Research involving computing.
   5. Scholars need to declare other Phd scholarships applied for at the
   time of applying and if selected by TCS, at the time of announcement.

We request the institute (new partners) to identify a single point of
contact (SPOC) for the institution. Other institutes can validate and
confirm the SPOC details.

*The SPOC will be responsible to ensure:*

entertained from scholars or any other person(s)).

b. Applications from the Institute are screened to filter and send us *top
best 5 applications only* *per institute**.*

c. Data shared in the applications/CV are complete and correct.
