Dear Students,

We are planning a certification course on "Quantum Computing : Systems and
Architecture". The course will deal with Quantum Computing more from the
Computer Systems perspective. The course description is attached.

The course will be offered by Prof. Kolin Paul (website
<https://kolinpaul.github.io/>) from IIT Delhi. He has contributed in
various fields within the Computer Systems domain like Computer
Architecture, Embedded Systems, Accelerator Design, High Performance
Computing, and Cyberphysical System Security. He is also a great teacher,
and this will be a nice opportunity to learn from him.

If you are interested in attending the course, please fill this form
<https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSesUJU4BorEoym8378LP6ug-Dzq8pjYoG9cQ7nbjJINVJ7mug/viewform?usp=publish-editor>.
Further communication will be restricted to those who fill the form. Please
fill the form by 26th January 5 PM.

Particulars:

   - The course will be open to all students who have done a course on
   Linear Algebra, done a course on programming, and have exposure to the idea
   of computing.
   - The course will have an evaluatory component in the form of small
   quizzes and/ or programming assignments. Students who successfully complete
   all the components will be issued certificates at the end of the course.
   (Students not interested in the certificate are also welcome :) )
   - It is a 10-hour course, spread across 5 days. The course dates are
   February 10th (Tuesday) to February 14th (Saturday). The classes from
   Tuesday to Friday will run from 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM, and the class on
   Saturday will run from 10:00 AM to 12:00 noon. (Note that the days may
   shift by a day or two based on the instructor's availability.)
   - The venue will be a classroom in the CLT. The course will be in
   offline mode only.
   - Please note that no credits can be earned through this course. This is
   purely for learning only!
